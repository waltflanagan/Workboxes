## 2026-01-13 — Relationship priorities framework

Framework for establishing what each person needs going forward — solvable vs. perpetual problems:

**Questions to work through:**
- What are the top priorities for you each going forward (relationship, co-parenting goals)?
- What is it each of you need to be healthy, happy, moving in the right direction?
- If you could break the cycle your relationship gets into, what would that look like?
- Can you name the dance you are in and stay out of defensive mode to problem-solve?
- What would it take to feel supported, to forgive, to start a new relationship?

**Structure: Negotiable vs. Non-Negotiable**

*Negotiable:* Some slip-ups in attention (with apology), no cheating/limited or no porn (with definition). Co-parenting consistency with flexibility. Balance life with work, kids, friends, time alone.

*Non-Negotiable:* Things returning to status quo after two weeks. Cheating (emotional, physical, psychological). Presence in the relationship (with expectations outlined). Trust — established and maintained. Emotional support (defined).

**Categories to assess:**
- Perpetual problems we may never solve
- Solvable problems we can work on
