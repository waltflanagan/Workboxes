# Relationship Commitments

## Roxy's Active Requests

Specific things Roxy has asked Mike for. Tracked from ask through resolution.

### Status key

- **open** — she has asked, Mike has not yet responded with action
- **in-progress** — Mike is actively working on it
- **met** — Mike and Roxy both agree it's been addressed
- **declined-by-mike** — Mike chose not to do this
- **withdrawn** — she has rescinded the request
- **unclear** — Mike isn't sure what exactly is being asked
