**SCHOOL INFORMATION**

**MAY READ ALOUD**

"Mixed Me!" by Taye Diggs is a heartwarming story that celebrates the beauty of diversity and promotes self-acceptance. This lesson plan is designed for students to explore themes of identity, diversity, and self-acceptance through interactive discussions, activities, and assessments. Teachers will read aloud this book in their classrooms during the week of May 11. Students will also write, on a cutout flower, about a time they showed perseverance. These will be displayed on the all-school tree by the library.

**3rd–5th GRADE TALENT SHOW**

The 3rd, 4th, and 5th Graders have the opportunity to perform in a live Talent Show in the Green gymnasium the morning of Friday, May 29. [This form](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Tmh14ao4xTXSzzW9N9RSI2lnC455qmTYMX4paJpB07Q/edit?usp=sharing) has been printed and shared with any students who have shown an interest in performing. Please make sure this form is completed and turned into Mrs. Cravotta by Monday, May 18. Parents of performers will receive a separate email invitation to attend this event.
