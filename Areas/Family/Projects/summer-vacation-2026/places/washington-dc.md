# Washington DC

## Overview
Smithsonian museums are free — one of the best family value days on the list.
Oscar and Nigel especially; Avery fine for a well-chosen museum or two.
1–2 days depending on appetite.

## Top picks (all Smithsonian = free admission)
- **Natural History** — dinosaurs, Hope Diamond, ocean hall; Avery-friendly
- **Air & Space** — Wright Brothers to Apollo; Oscar and Nigel
- **American History** — pop culture, First Ladies, americana
- **National Zoo** — free; solid half-day
- **monuments loop** — Lincoln, Vietnam Wall, WWII, Jefferson; walkable

## Logistics
- Parking in DC is painful — strongly prefer metro or Amtrak in
- From Goshen area: ~4 hrs drive; could overnight nearby (Maryland or Virginia suburbs)
- Metro from suburban stations is easy once in the area

## Est. cost (family of 5)
Admissions free; transport + food + 1 night nearby: ~$200–400
