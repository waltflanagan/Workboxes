# Niagara Falls, NY

## Overview
Iconic; genuinely worth it. Works as an overnight stop in Buffalo with a
morning visit before continuing east.

## Key attractions
- **Maid of the Mist** — boat ride to the base of the falls; ~$26–30/person
- **Cave of the Winds** — walkway to the base of Bridal Veil Falls; ~$20–25/person
- **Combo ticket** — both attractions; est. family of 5: ~$200–250
- Canadian side (Horseshoe Falls) is more dramatic but requires passports

## Logistics
- Passports needed if crossing to Canadian side
- American side attractions are solid without crossing
- Overnight Buffalo: budget hotel ~$100–150/night
