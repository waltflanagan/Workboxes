# Goshen / Hudson Valley, NY

Trip anchor. Legoland is here; several strong add-ons within 30 min.

## Legoland NY
- 1 Legoland Blvd, Goshen, NY 10924
- Avery's wheelhouse; 2 days recommended
- Waterpark combo available; on-site waterpark good for July heat
- Tickets: dynamic pricing — buy 30+ days out to save 20–30%
- Est. family of 5: ~$375–650 depending on days and timing
- [legoland.com/new-york](https://www.legoland.com/new-york/)

### Stay options
- **On-site hotel** — early entry (30 min), themed rooms, characters at breakfast; ~$300–500/night peak summer; book early
- **Short-term rental (Warwick / Goshen / Middletown)** — more space, better value for 3+ nights, 10–20 min drive; ~$150–250/night

## Storm King Art Center
- 1 Museum Rd, New Windsor, NY 12553 (~15 min from Legoland)
- 500-acre outdoor sculpture park; genuinely worth it
- Adult ~$22, Child (5–12) ~$10; family of 5 est. ~$70–80
- [stormking.org](https://stormking.org)

## Harriman State Park
- Seven Lakes Dr, Harriman, NY (~20 min from Legoland)
- 200+ miles of trails, lakes, swimming holes; parking ~$10 in summer
- Pine Meadow Lake: easy family hike with a payoff swim

## West Point
- 2107 New South Post Rd, West Point, NY (~20 min from Legoland)
- Free tours of military academy grounds; museum, Trophy Point overlook
- Oscar-friendly; pair with a Hudson River walk in Cold Spring or Beacon
