# D&D Club Logistics

**Attendance:** Club attendance is flexible. Drop in/Drop out.

**Band App:** Students are welcome to join the MMS D&D Band with their own accounts. Materials like character sheets and info about creating adventures will be shared there. Moderated chat rooms for kids to connect outside of club. Students under 13 can join with parental consent.

**Volunteering:** Always looking for more volunteers — both with experience to help teach/mentor and adults to help manage behaviors. Completing the ELPS volunteer form is required: https://elps.us/our-district/district-forms-and-documents/

Volunteer roles:
- Running Games
- Shadow DMing (helping with rules/table management)
- Helping with Setup/Teardown/Crowd Control
- Helping outside of club in some other way

**Supplies:** Enough dice for everyone but only ~4 of each Player's Handbook and Monster Manual to share amongst 40+ students.

Amazon supply list: https://www.amazon.com/registries/gl/guest-view/1T5I861TQUT4X

For students getting their own supplies: 7-piece RPG dice set + D&D Player's Handbook (2024 version of 5E, not the 2014 version).
