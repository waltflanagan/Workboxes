# Maryland — Chesapeake Bay

## Overview
Steamed blue crabs, Old Bay, picnic tables, cold drinks. Classic summer
experience. More of a meal/half-day than a full destination — pairs naturally
with DC or a beach stop.

## Best spots
- **Annapolis** — charming waterfront city; good crab houses; Naval Academy nearby
- **Kent Island / Grasonville** — Eastern Shore entry point off the Bay Bridge; casual waterfront
- **Crisfield** — deep Eastern Shore; serious crab town, less touristy

## Timing
Peak blue crab season is July–September — timing is right.

## Est. cost (family of 5)
Crabs by the dozen: ~$150–250 depending on market price + restaurant
