## Legoland NY — tickets

Family of 5 (2 adults + 3 kids):

| Option | Est. cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1-day general admission | ~$375–500 | Adult ~$85–95, Child ~$75–85; prices vary by date |
| 2-day ticket | ~$500–650 | Better per-day value; check multi-day discount |
| Waterpark combo | +$50–100 | Add-on to park ticket |
| Season pass | ~$900+ | Only worth it if visiting twice |

> 💡 Legoland uses dynamic pricing — summer weekends are peak. Buying in advance
> (30+ days out) typically saves 20–30%.

## EV charging estimate

**Vehicle:** Tesla Model Y
**Charging:** Tesla Supercharger network (dense coverage along I-90, I-76, I-78, and Northeast corridor)

| Scenario | Miles | Est. kWh used | Est. Supercharger cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| Straight shot each way | ~1,160 mi | ~330 kWh | ~$85–100 |
| With detours / stops | ~1,400 mi | ~400 kWh | ~$100–120 |

*Assumes ~3.5 mi/kWh highway efficiency (conservative for loaded Model Y in summer).
Supercharger rate ~$0.25–0.30/kWh. Destination charging at hotel may offset some cost.*

**Charging stops:** Model Y Long Range (~330 mi range) will need 1–2 Supercharger stops each way
depending on route. Tesla nav handles this automatically.
