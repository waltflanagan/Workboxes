# Erie, PA

## Presque Isle State Park
- 301 Peninsula Dr, Erie, PA 16505
- Barrier peninsula with beaches, kayaking, bike trails, birding
- Free admission; PA state parks charge no entry fee
- Good low-key driving break; half-day stretch

## Notes
- Busier on summer weekends; arrive early for parking
- Beach swimming areas are lifeguarded in season
